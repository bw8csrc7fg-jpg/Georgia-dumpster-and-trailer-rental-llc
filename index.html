Frequently asked questions

What can I put in the dump trailer or dumpster?
Most household junk, furniture, yard waste, and construction debris are fine. We cannot take paint, oils, fuel, chemicals, tires, or other hazardous materials. Ask us if you’re not sure.
Do you charge sales tax on the rental?
Yes. Georgia requires sales tax on the rental of tangible property (like trailers and dumpsters). Your quote will clearly show the rental price, estimated dump fees, and applicable sales tax.
How much driveway space do I need?
You’ll need enough space for the trailer/dumpster and our truck to safely maneuver. A typical driveway works fine if it’s clear, fairly level, and free of low branches or wires. We can discuss placement before we deliver.
